Closer Click — Identity Vault

This page is the cross-app storage for Closer Click identities. It is meant to be embedded as an invisible iframe by the apps.

Stored in localStorage on this origin:

  • Your ECDSA P-256 keypair (private key never leaves this page).
  • Nicknames and ratings of peers you've met.

Source: github.com/seyacat/closer-click-identity
